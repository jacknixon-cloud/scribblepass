Math Cards Help & Support

How to Play

Math Cards presents math problems as colorful playing cards. Select a game mode from the home screen, choose your difficulty level (for fraction modes), and start practicing! Answer each problem by entering your solution. Get immediate feedback with visual and sound effects.

Game Modes Explained

Addition (FREE)

Practice addition problems to build your fundamental math skills. This mode is completely free forever!

Subtraction (Subscription)

Practice subtraction problems to strengthen your understanding of taking away numbers.

Multiplication Tables (Subscription)

Practice multiplication facts for tables 1 through 10, or challenge yourself with all tables combined.

Division Tables (Subscription)

Practice division with divisors 1 through 10. Problems include visual dot representations to help understand the concepts. Larger dividends may display across multiple cards.

Simplify Fractions (Subscription)

Reduce fractions to their lowest terms. Available in Easy, Medium, and Hard difficulty levels.

Multiply Fractions (Subscription)

Multiply two fractions together. Choose from three difficulty levels based on your skill.

Divide Fractions (Subscription)

Divide fractions with mixed number answers. Watch the animated reciprocal visualization to understand the process. Three difficulty levels available.

Compare Fractions (Subscription)

Identify which of two fractions is larger. Practice with Easy, Medium, or Hard problems.

Add Fractions (Subscription)

Add two fractions together. Select your difficulty level and practice finding common denominators.

Subtract Fractions (Subscription)

Subtract one fraction from another. Available in three difficulty levels.

Features

Work Pad

The Work Pad is an educational tool that provides step-by-step guidance for solving fraction problems. It breaks down complex operations into manageable steps, including finding greatest common divisors, calculating least common denominators, and simplifying results. Solutions automatically populate the answer fields, but you can solve problems directly without using this feature.

Hall of Fame

Track your progress with leaderboards for each game mode. See your rankings with scores and trophy icons for top performers. All scores are saved locally on your device.

Difficulty Levels

Each fraction game mode offers three difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard) to provide appropriate challenge progression as your skills improve.

Scoring System

  • Correct Answer: Earn 100 points, see green "Correct!" text, confetti animation, and hear a ding sound
  • Incorrect Answer: See red "Wrong!" text with buzzer sound. Your answer is displayed for review, but no points are deducted
  • Leaderboards: Compete for top scores in each game mode

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Math Cards free?

Addition mode is completely free forever! You can practice addition problems at no cost. To unlock all other game modes (subtraction, multiplication, division, and all fraction modes), a monthly subscription is required.

What's included in the subscription?

A subscription unlocks all 9 premium game modes: Subtraction, Multiplication Tables, Division Tables, Simplify Fractions, Multiply Fractions, Divide Fractions, Compare Fractions, Add Fractions, and Subtract Fractions. You'll also get access to all difficulty levels and the Work Pad feature for fraction problems.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your App Store account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

Does Math Cards need an internet connection?

No! After initial installation, Math Cards works completely offline. All game data and scores are stored locally on your device.

What ages is Math Cards designed for?

Math Cards is designed for elementary and middle school students, approximately ages 7-13, learning basic arithmetic and fraction operations.

Can I use Math Cards on iPad?

Currently, Math Cards is optimized for iPhone only and is not compatible with iPad.

Will my scores be saved?

Yes! All scores and Hall of Fame leaderboards are saved locally on your device and persist between sessions.

Can multiple children use the same device?

Yes! Each player can enter their own name for leaderboard entries, allowing multiple children to track their individual progress.

Troubleshooting

The app isn't loading

Try closing and reopening the app. If problems persist, restart your device.

Sound effects aren't playing

Check that your device isn't in silent mode and that the volume is turned up. Sound effects are synthesized in real-time using Web Audio API.

My scores disappeared

Scores are stored locally. If you cleared your browser data or app cache, scores may have been deleted. Scores cannot be recovered once deleted.
