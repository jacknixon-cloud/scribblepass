Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Scribble Pass does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game data (drawings, player names, settings) is stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

Scribble Pass uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Displays ads for free users. AdMob may collect device information for ad serving. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store: Processes in-app purchases. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Player name
  • Game settings (drawing mode, timer preferences)
  • Premium purchase status
  • Tutorial completion status

This data never leaves your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

Scribble Pass does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is designed to be family-friendly and does not require any personal information to play.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
